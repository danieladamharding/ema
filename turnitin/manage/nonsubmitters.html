Emailing Students Who Haven't Submitted
  • If you have set up a Turnitin dropbox where a reminder is needed or students have failed to submit, you can use the Email non-submitters option to contact them.

  • Click the Email non-submitters option.

  • Begin to type your message, including a subject line and main body of the email.

  • Click the Include me option if you would like a copy of the email to be sent to yourself.
  • Click the Send button to email students who've yet to submit.
All done! Your students will receive an email reminding them to submit their assignment.
