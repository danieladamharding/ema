Turnitin backups should be made once the assignment has been marked and completed.
Step 1 - Select your Turnitin dropbox
  • Sign in to the Keele Learning Environment (KLE) and navigate to the module containing your Turnitin dropbox.
  • From the menu on the left, navigate to the Course Management section.
  • From the Course Management section select Course Tools.
  • Select Turnitin Assignments.
  • Choose the appropriate dropbox link from the list.
Step 2 - Preparing your download
  • From within the Turnitin inbox, click the checkbox next to Author to select all of the submissions.
  • Click the download button on the right hand side.
  • Select either Original File or Grademark Paper.

    • Some schools may wish to download both the original and GradeMark papers - please refer to existing School practice.

  • Once ready, the Messages tab will display a 1 New alert.
Step 3 - Downloading your submissions
  • Click on the Messages tab.
  • You will see a list of pending downloads, with the most recent listed as New!.
  • Click on the message you wish to download the archived dropbox for.
  • You will be taken to a confirmation screen. Click on the link to download the file.
  • A zip file containing the Turnitin documents will start to download.

    • This file will be available for 7 days at which point it will be removed.

Step 4 - Storing your Turnitin backups
  • Once you have downloaded the zipped file containing the submissions, you will need to store them safely.
  • Move the .zip file(s) to a secure University network drive, ideally a shared office drive.
  • Once you have copied your file to a shared drive, it will be automatically backed up by University IT Services.
Alright! You have successfully backed up your Turnitin assignment dropbox.
