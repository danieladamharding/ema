Understanding the Turnitin Inbox (3 of 3)
Returns the instructor to the main Assignment Inbox view.
Allows you to change assignment settings such as the post date, assignment title etc. It is recommended that settings are not adjusted after submissions have been received due to previous issues relating to the release of feedback.
Manage your QuickMark and rubric libraries, including the ability to import and export both types of sets.
View and download detailed information relating to QuickMark use, rubrics and text matches across all submissions.
A limited amount of preference settings are available, for example, to adjust inbox pagination and default download formats.

    Hover over the highlighted areas above to reveal their function.
  1. Assignment Inbox: Returns the instructor to the main Assignment Inbox view.
  2. Edit assignment: Allows you to change assignment settings such as the post date, assignment title etc. It is recommended that settings are not adjusted after submissions have been received due to previous issues relating to the release of feedback.
  3. Libraries: Manage your QuickMark and rubric libraries, including the ability to import and export both types of sets.
  4. Class Stats: View and download detailed information relating to QuickMark use, rubrics and text matches across all submissions.
  5. Preferences: A limited amount of preference settings are available, for example, to adjust inbox pagination and default download formats.
