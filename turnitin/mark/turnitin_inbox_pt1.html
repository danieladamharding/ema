Understanding the Turnitin Inbox (1 of 3)

    Image too small? Click here to enlarge.

    Hover over the highlighted areas above to reveal their function.
  1. Submit: Allows the tutor to submit an assignment on behalf of students.
  2. Roster Sync: Synchronises the list of students between Blackboard and Turnitin. Can also be used to populate missing students who haven't appeared in the Turnitin inbox.
  3. Edit assignment settings: Return to the Turnitin assignment settings.
  4. Email non-submitters: Using the 'Email non-submitters' option will send an email to all students who have yet to submit their assignment via Turnitin.
