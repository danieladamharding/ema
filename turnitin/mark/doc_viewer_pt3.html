Understanding the Turnitin Document Viewer (3 of 3)
QuickMarks are customisable sets of short drag and drop feedback comments. This tab allows you to manage your QuickMarks and apply them to an assignment.
Provides a space to write general assignment feedback of up to 5000 characters. Audio feedback of up to 3 minutes can also be recorded directly from your device.
A summary of all QuickMarks and comments applied to the current assignment will appear here.
Rubrics are a customisable marking grid. There are standard rubrics (automatically generates a grade), qualitative (provide detailed information about the grade achieved but the marker decides on the overall mark) or customisable (a mark can be assigned for each point on the scale). This tab also allows the marker to complete a grading form, if one has been applied to the assignment.

    Hover over the highlighted areas above to reveal their function.
  1. Add QuickMarks or comments: QuickMarks are customisable sets of short drag and drop feedback comments. This tab allows you to manage your QuickMarks and apply them to an assignment.
  2. View/edit general comments: Provides a space to write general assignment feedback of up to 5000 characters. Audio feedback of up to 3 minutes can also be recorded directly from your device.
  3. View all comments and QuickMarks: A summary of all QuickMarks and comments applied to the current assignment will appear here.
  4. View/edit rubric scorecard: Rubrics are a customisable marking grid containing criteria agreed for the assignment. Types include:
    • Standard rubrics: Automatically generate a grade.
    • Qualitative rubrics: Provide detailed information about the grade, but the marker decides on the overall mark.
    • Customisable rubrics: A mark can be assigned for each point on the scale.

    This tab also allows the marker to complete a grading form, if one has been applied to the assignment.
